Our Features

Line

The Merriam-Webster definition of “line” comprises 15 parts, 46 sections, and 41 subsections.

Shape

When a line, or more than one line, closes or connects, a shape is formed.Shapes are two-dimensional. They can be measured by overall height and width.

Form

The Merriam-Webster definition of “form” comprises 12 parts, 27 sections, and 4 subsections. Form is three-dimensional. Form has overall height, width, and depth.

Texture

Texture in the photograph is similar to form in that it is revealed by variations in tonality and presented in two dimensions.

color

Light itself has no perceived color. But, send light through a prism or a drop of water and we can see that it is comprised of a literal rainbow of colors. Color has three properties: hue, value, and saturation.

Size and Depth

Size in a photograph is relative and can be an illusion.
In photography, the space is already rendered before the camera, so we look at how size and depth are reproduced, created, and recognized in the photograph.

Photography

Photography deals exquisitely with appearances, but nothing is what it appears to be.
